KM-X3L

Portable Oxygen Concentrator

Total portability, compact and extremely quiet operation.

Benefits

Homecare oxygen therapy supportive device. Battery-powered, compact, lightweight and extremely quiet. Our Oxygen Concentrator can be carried in the front passenger seat of a vehicle or in a special carrying cart.

Quality Certificates and Rewards

Technical Specifications

Special features

2 modes of oxygen delivery – continuous and pulse mode

Extremely sensitive breath trigger for even the lightest breath

Stylish, compact and extremely quiet operation

Less than 33 dBA at maximum setting

Guaranteed bolus volume delivery

Up to 40 breaths per minute and 90% purity – up to 100ml per breath

Total portability

Optional battery provides over 2 hours of operation at maximum setting. KMX-3L can go with you everywhere. Battery can be recharged using KMX-3L car adaptor (Option).
