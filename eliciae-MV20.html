Eliciae MV20

Respiratory ventilator ELICIAE MV20

Robust CMV ventilation based on proprietary pressure-controlled ventilation technology.

Robust and easy to transport

Steel outer casing ensures robustness for use in busy wards or temporary facilities. With a main unit of just 6kg, the device can be moved easily

Simple to use

3 main ventilation modes and high visibility LED numerial display, so that users with less experience can manage the settings easily and quickly. Mandatory ventilation is pressure based, reducing risk of barotrauma associated with volume-controlled ventilation. Back-up mode provides weaning support, and Monitoring Mode enables further weaning with CPAP support.

Low cost and high manufacturability

Focusing on core ventilation functionality and avoiding features that many users never touch, we have been able to control manufacturing costs and provide a reasonably priced product with special value for countries fighting against the COVID-19 pandemic.
